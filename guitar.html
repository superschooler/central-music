Guitar Lessons
Beginning/Intermediate - All Ages

Our guitar lessons offer a full range of kid friendly lessons to adult refresher for all ages and abilities. Our University trained teachers will help you learn guitar in a style that caters to your musical interest and unleashes your creativity. Whether you are a novice or expert, we have a lesson suited for you. Progress is catered to each student, allowing for maximum attention and advancement. Our teachers want you to learn guitar that you feel passionate about, so be sure to tell them what style of music you enjoy!

Guitar (Acoustic/Electric) lessons for beginners focus on finger placement, strumming, sight-reading, understanding groove and chord placement. Students will learn to care for their instrument as well as tuning the guitar. From classic rock to current music, guitar lessons are sure to excite everyone.

Our intermediate lessons add chord progressions, finger picking, pentatonic scales, ear training and reading sheet music.

For the advanced student we offer lessons that teach scale sequencing, sweep picking, riffs and intervals.
