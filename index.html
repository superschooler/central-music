about us

Central Music is the inspiration of a local Fillmore family. We wanted to bring something positive and productive to our community. When people of all ages take music lessons, they learn much more the music and how to perform.
Did you know, Music helps develop skills needed in today's work force: critical thinking, creative problem solving, effective communication, teamwork, and confidence. Kids in music lessons are more engaged in school and less likely to drop out while helping them achieve in other academic subjects like math, science, and reading. Music also helps communities share ideas and values among cultures and generations.
Music lessons are valuable for all ages. Parents find that music lessons for kids not only improves their children't memorization and small-motor skills, but strongly contribute to building of their child's self-confidence. Adults see music lessons as a creative outlet and something to help them de-stress after a long day as well as being a way to express their emotions. Older adults (seniors) find that music lessons are a great way to stay mentally active - music is known to be therapeutic and a great way to keep one's mind young!
