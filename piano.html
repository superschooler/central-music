Piano Lessons
Beginning/Intermediate Piano - All Ages

Private piano lessons are offered for all ages. It is recommended students have a piano or keyboard at home for practice. Instruction is on traditional acoustic pianos and can be provided as needed on digital MIDI instruments.

This class is for those who know very little about music! If you are just starting out or want a review of the very basics, this course is for you! This class will cover introductory subjects as well as playing simple examples on the piano. If you have never touched a piano before, this is where you want to start.

Lessons normally help children find notes on the piano, learn how to read music, play simple rhythms and songs, play with simple dynamics, and find music to play.

Interested piano students have frequent performance opportunities in recitals, festivals and/or contests.
